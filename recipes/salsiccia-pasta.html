Salsiccia Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • Salsiccia Sausage
  • garlic
  • tomato paste
  • Mezze Maniche or other pasta of your liking
  • Cream
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking

  1. Chop the garlic and add them to a small bowl
  2. Cut the sausages open and get all the meat out of the sausages
  3. Bring you water to a boil. Proceed with the next step and just add salt and pasta when the water starts boiling
  4. Add your salsiccia to a medium-heated pan
  5. As the fat starts melting, bring the heat up
  6. As the salsiccia gets done, add some tomato paste (straight to the pan, not in the salsiccia)
  7. Stir fry and then add the Cream
  8. Let that simmer and then mix togheter the pastsa with your salissica.
  9. ENJOY
