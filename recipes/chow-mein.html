Chow Mein (Stir Fried Chicken Wok)

Description

Ingredients

  • One yellow onion
  • Three cloves of garlic
  • 2 carrots
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 300 grams of Egg Noodles
  • 150 grams of boneless chicken thighs
  • Bright chinese soy sauce
  • Dark Chinese soy sauce (A tad less)
  • Chicken powder
  • White pepper

Cooking

  1. Boil the noodles using the instructions on the package
  2. Wash the noodles in cold water and put them on a plate with some oil, making sure that they are seperated to some degree
  3. Whisk the eggs together in a bowl and add some chicken powder and white pepper
  4. Chop the onion and carrots, chop the garlic. Add it to a bowl (you will soon find out why all the bowls are crucial)
  5. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces
  6. Fire up your wok, add a good amount of oil and fry a very soft omelette with your eggs, and then pour them back to the old bowl
  7. Bring the wok back to a high temperature, add some oil, and add your vegetables until they get a golden brown colour. And then pour them back to their bowl
  8. Do the same with the chicken, but make sure to season it and NOT to add it to the old bowl, but bring out a new one instead
  9. And now for the noodles. Add the soy sauce and season to your liking and whisk together with all the other ingredients.
Pro tip: To get an even amount of sauce on your dish, pour the sauce in on the side of the wok and then toss together.
